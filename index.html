Hi, I'm Songtao Mao. I'm currently a second-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at the Johns Hopkins University, advised by Prof. Xin Li. Prior to this, I earned my Bachelor of Science degree in Mathematics from Zhiyuan College at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My primary research focus is on theoretical computer science, particularly in the areas of randomized algorithms and pseudorandomness, coding theory, and computational complexity. Additionally, I have a keen interest in cryptography and quantum computing. Feel free to reach out to me anytime.
